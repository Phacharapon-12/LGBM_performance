Key Economic Relationships Discovered by the Model
🇰🇷
Korea Cycle → Thai Business Growth
Strongest single relationship found
Korea CLI Momentum ──▶ Thai Sector Growth
Korea's economic leading indicator — which measures manufacturing orders and export activity — predicts Thai business growth 1 quarter ahead. The supply chain link between Korean industry and Thai production creates a reliable early warning signal.
Signal strength: 8% of total predictive power for 1Q forecast — largest single macro signal in the model.
🌐
G7 + US Cycle → Demand Pipeline
Global demand leads Thai exports
G7 / US Leading Indicators ──▶ Export-linked Sectors
When G7 economies expand, demand for Thai exports follows within 1–2 quarters. The model captures this lag and uses it to forecast which sectors — electronics, automotive, food — benefit most from the global upswing.
Signal strength: Combined G7 + US CLI signals contribute ~12% of 1Q predictive power.
📈
Price Momentum → Near-term Growth
Markets embed forward-looking information
52W / 13W Returns ──▶ Earnings Trajectory
Stock prices aggregate expectations of thousands of analysts and investors. The model learns that sustained price momentum over 13 and 52 weeks is a powerful predictor of actual revenue and earnings growth in the near term.
Signal strength: Momentum signals account for ~60% of 1Q predictive power — the market's own consensus is the strongest signal.
🇹🇭
Thai Macro → Long-term Trajectory
Becomes critical for 1-year horizon
GDP + CPI + Exports ──▶ Year-ahead Business Growth
For 1-year forecasts, domestic fundamentals become far more important. GDP growth, inflation, tourist arrivals and current account data combine to map where Thai corporate earnings will be in 4 quarters.
Signal strength: Thai macro rises from 8% (1Q) to 35% of 4Q predictive power — a fundamental shift between horizons that the model learns automatically.
🏭
Global Sector Rotation → Thai Sectors
International capital flows as early signal
US Sector ETF Flows ──▶ Corresponding Thai Sectors
When global investors rotate into biotech, robotics, or utilities in the US, similar moves follow in emerging markets including Thailand. The model detects these sector rotation patterns weeks before they fully materialise in Thai stocks.
Signal strength: Key for 4Q model — Biotech & Robotics ETF returns among top 20 signals for year-ahead prediction.
